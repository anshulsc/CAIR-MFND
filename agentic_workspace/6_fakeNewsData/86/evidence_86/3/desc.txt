Mt. <strong>Kailash</strong> <strong>is</strong> a peak in the Kailas Range <strong>in</strong> <strong>Tibet</strong>. It lies near the source of some of the longest Asian rivers: the Brahmaputra, the Sutlej and the Karnali. It is considered sacred in four religions: Bon, Buddhism, Hinduism, and Janism.
